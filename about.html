Our Mission

CyberSafe in the Community is dedicated to bridging the cybersecurity knowledge gap. We believe that everyone, regardless of age or tech-savviness, deserves access to practical, easy-to-understand cybersecurity education.

We focus on:

  • Making cybersecurity education accessible and practical
  • Focusing on real-world threats and practical solutions
  • Empowering older adults with the knowledge to stay safe online
  • Creating a community where people feel confident with technology
  • Providing free, comprehensive resources for everyone
